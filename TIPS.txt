This file will contain tips, on 5W's of "Solving a CP question"

Reading process: 
1.Read the question.
2.Understand the question.
3.Read multiple times the question with an eye on the "Sample inputs" and "Sample outputs".

Solution process :
1.Observe patter from the "diagram" (if given), "inputs and outputs"
2.Then come up with a solution.
3.Break the solution into pieces of solution.
4.Solve each part of the solution
5.Combine all the parts of the solution (using common sense logic)
